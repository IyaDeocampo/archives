Women and Baskets

Anita Magsaysay-Ho (1914–2012) was a pioneering Filipina modernist painter, celebrated for her depictions of women engaged in everyday tasks such as fishing, weaving, and market life. As the only female member of the “Thirteen Moderns,” she blended Social Realism and post-Cubism to honor Filipino women’s strength, community, and artistry.
